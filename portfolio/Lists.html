Sieve Analysis in Soil Mechanics

Sieve Analysis to Determine Grain Size of Soil

Procedures

  1. Collect a representative dry soil sample.
  2. Weigh the soil sample accurately.
  3. Arrange the sieves in descending order of size .
  4. Place the soil on the top sieve.
  5. Fix the sieve stack in the sieve shaker.
  6. Shake the sieve for about 10-15 minutes.
  7. Weigh the soil retained on each sieve.
  8. Calculate the percentage of soil retained and passing

Apparatus used

  • IS Sieves
  • Sieve Shaker
  • Weighing Balance
  • Oven
  • Tray
  • Brush

Description

IS Sieves
Used to separate soil particles based on size.
Weighing Balance
Used to measuere the weight of soil samples accurately.
Oven
Used to dry the soil sample at a controlled temperature.
Brush
Used to clean the sieves after testing.
